
rc_tut is a map made by Creme/X-Tender for the HLRally TDK (Track Developers Kit)
It was packed with an RMF file that was missing a bunch of different things, so I tried fixing it. - b1rd

info_camera acts as the reference point for the stationary camera mode. 
info_waypoints are used by bots to follow the track.
info_checkpoints brushes need to touch the ground, if they don't they won't count when you go through them (i think)

rc_tut - Thursday, 30 May, 2002 - Creme/X-Tender
============================

Tutorial track for HL-Rally.
Learn how to use info_checkpoints and how to creatively use 
trianges to make landscape and curves.
